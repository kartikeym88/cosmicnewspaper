Welcome to The Cosmic Times

Step into a world where the cosmos meets journalism. The Cosmic Times is your personalized space newspaper, bringing you the wonders of the universe from any day in history. Whether you're curious about what happened in space on your birthday, a significant date, or just want to explore the astronomical events of the past, we've got you covered.

Our digital newspaper combines NASA's stunning Astronomy Picture of the Day with real space mission data, creating a unique reading experience that's both educational and visually captivating.

How It Works

Simply select any date below and click "Launch Edition" to generate your personalized cosmic newspaper. Each edition includes the NASA Astronomy Picture of the Day for that date, any space launches or events that occurred, and information about upcoming missions.

The data is sourced from NASA's official APIs and The Space Devs launch library, ensuring you get accurate and up-to-date information about humanity's greatest adventure - space exploration.
